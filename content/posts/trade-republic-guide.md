### Core Features Breakdown

#### 1. The Savings Plan (Sparplan)
This is the crown jewel. You can set up recurring investments into significantly thousands of ETFs (like the popular MSCI World) or individual stocks.
*   **Cost:** Free.
*   **Minimum:** Just €1.
*   **Frequency:** Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

#### 2. The Trade Republic Card
More than just a debit card, it connects your spending to your investing.
*   **Saveback:** Earn 1% Saveback rewards on card payments, invested directly into a savings plan of your choice.
*   **Round up:** Automatically round up your spare change from purchases and invest the difference.
*   **No monthly fee:** The virtual card is free; physical cards have a one-time cost.

#### 3. Security and Regulation
Trade Republic isn't just a startup; it holds a full German banking license.
*   **Deposit Protection:** Your cash is protected up to €100,000 (typical EU deposit guarantee schemes).
*   **Regulation:** Supervised by BaFin (Germany) and the Bundesbank.

### Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
*   Extremely easy to use interface.
*   No order fees for savings plans.
*   High interest on cash balance.
*   Local tax reports provided (especially helpful in Germany).

**Cons:**
*   Limited analysis tools for advanced traders.
*   Customer support is primarily via email/app.
*   Only one trading venue (Lang & Schwarz Exchange) - though spreads are monitored.

### How to Get Started

1.  **Download the App:** Available on iOS and Android.
2.  **Verify Identity:** Use VideoIdent or PostIdent. It usually takes less than 10 minutes.
3.  **Deposit Funds:** Transfer money via bank transfer, Apple Pay, or credit card (instant options might have a small fee).
4.  **Create a Savings Plan:** Search for an ETF (e.g., "Core MSCI World"), select "Savings Plan", choose your interval and amount. Done.

### Final Thoughts

Trade Republic is our top recommendation for anyone looking to *simplify* their financial life. by automating investments, you remove the emotional stress of trading and build strict habits effortlessly.
