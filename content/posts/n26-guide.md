## Key Features and Benefits

N26 packs plenty of functionality into its sleek interface. Here are the highlights that help European customers **save time**, fees, and stress.

### 1. Fast, Paperless Onboarding

Opening an account takes around 10 minutes with your smartphone. Scan your ID, record a quick video call for verification, and your virtual card appears instantly in the app. Physical cards typically arrive within a few days, so you can start spending online or in-store right away.

### 2. Spaces for Effortless Budgeting

Spaces are sub-accounts that let you allocate money for rent, travel, or savings goals. Drag-and-drop transfers make it simple to stash funds the moment you get paid, while Shared Spaces allow joint saving with family or flatmates. Automatic rounding and rules in premium plans help you grow savings without manual transfers.

### 3. Real-Time Notifications and Analytics

Every transaction triggers an instant push notification, so you always know where your money is going. Category tagging and monthly insights turn your spending history into actionable data, helping you spot subscriptions you no longer use or areas where you can cut back.

### 4. Travel-Friendly Perks

All accounts include fee-free payments worldwide, while premium tiers add free ATM withdrawals abroad, built-in travel insurance, and dedicated customer support lines. You can lock or unlock your card in the app, set spending limits, and generate single-use virtual cards for extra security when booking accommodation or flights online.

### 5. Integrations That Make Life Easier

Sync N26 with Apple Pay or Google Pay in seconds, connect it to budgeting apps like YNAB, or export transactions via CSV for tax prep. Freelancers love that they can receive SEPA transfers, get paid in different currencies (converted at Mastercard rates), and generate invoices directly from their account details.
