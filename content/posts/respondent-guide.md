### How to get picked more often

Treat Respondent like a professional marketplace. Flesh out your work history, certifications, and tools you use daily. When a screener asks for specifics, add quantifiable results—hiring managers see the detail and fast-track your application. I keep a short template ready so each application takes minutes, not half an hour.

### What payout looks like

Once a researcher marks your session complete, the payout clock starts. Most of my sessions have landed in PayPal inside five business days, and the dashboard shows a status for every project so you are never guessing. It feels transparent compared with platforms that bury you in support tickets.
